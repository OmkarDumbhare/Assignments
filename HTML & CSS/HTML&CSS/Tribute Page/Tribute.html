About STEPHEN CURRY

☛Wardell Stephen Curry II (born March 14, 1988) is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA).
☛Curry was selected by the Warriors with the seventh overall pick in the 2009 NBA Draft.
☛In 2014–15, Curry won his first NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) award and led the Warriors to their first championship since 1975.
☛he following season, he became the first player in NBA history to be elected MVP by a unanimous vote and was the NBA annual scoring leader while shooting above 50–40–90.
☛List of career achievements by Stephen Curry

  • 3 time NBA champion: 2015,2017,2018
  • 1st All-time in 3 pointers made in NBA history.
  • 2x NBA Most Valuable Player: 2015, 2016
  • NBA All-Star Game MVP 2022
  • 8x NBA All-Star: 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2021, 2022
  • 2× NBA scoring leader: 2016, 2021
  • 2× NBA Three-Point Contest champion: 2015, 2021
  • Warriors franchise leader in Points
  • Warriors franchise leader in Assists
  • 2x ESPY Award for Best Male Athlete and Best NBA Player (2015)(2021)
  • 4x BET Award for Sportsman of the Year (2015-2017) (2019)
  • Lead the Warriors to a 73-9 record in the NBA
☛Stephen Curry is renowned for his shooting accuracy and for leading the league for the most number of three-pointers during three seasons. He set the record with 272 three-pointers during the 2012–13 season and then surpassed his own record in 2015 and 2016 with 286 and 402 three-pointers respectively.
